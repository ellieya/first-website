Introduction

It's a good thing this project is so lenient in terms of content, because all of the content on this page was written from midnight to 3AM. It's difficult for me to come up with things to talk about myself. So, uhm...expect pointless rambling & tangents. The text on this website is pretty worthless, it might as well be lorem ipsum.

So what is the significance of the image on the right side of this page? There is none. I just so happened to be watching Jojo, and I told my friend it would look nice for a background image and he agreed, so yeah...

Er, well it isn't a background image in this instance. That's because I wanted a dark theme. So it's a side image!

What was I thinking when I came up with this color scheme? Hmm, well, I played MapleStory2 a long time ago, and many people were walking around with hairs using purple/pink w/ cyan gradients. At first I was like, "pffffffft, overrated," but you know, it is pretty attractive.

Playspan used similar colors, if I recall correctly. I don't know how many of you played MMORPG back when Grand Chase/MapleStory (RIP Grand Chase) ruled over F2P MMORPG, but Playspan were the guys who made Ultimate Game Card. Yeah, I have no idea what they're doing nowathesedays. I think they're bankrupt? I have no idea how that would happen, though. Oh, Visa bought them out. Well, good for them.

The general layout...well, I don't really like it. The reason for this is because it looks a bit like a typical tumblr design. (I have horrible memories about that site...) I'd rather have a more dynamic website, but my progress on learning JavaScript isn't going very well. Though, I suppose nobody can actually learn enough JavaScript to do what I want to do in 3 days.

Unless they pulled 3 all-nighters in a row, I guess. But then how would you concentrate? Well, I suppose a genius could do it.

Anyways, to sum it up, this project is nowhere close to the maximum of my current capabilities. For future reference, I will focus more on content before structure on the website, and build it around that, rather than the other way around.
